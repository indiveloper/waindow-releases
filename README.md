# Waindow for Mac

Official download page for Waindow by indiveloper.

Waindow helps you keep busy Mac workspaces under control when AI tools, terminals, notes, browser windows, and captures are all open at once.

## Download

[Download Waindow for Mac](https://github.com/indiveloper/waindow-releases/releases/download/v1.0.0/Waindow-1.0.0.dmg)

- 14-day free trial
- No card required to start
- macOS 13 or later
- Works on Apple Silicon and Intel Macs

## Install

1. Download Waindow for Mac.
2. Open the downloaded file.
3. Drag Waindow into Applications.
4. Open Waindow from Applications.

## Why Waindow

- Find the run that is waiting for you
- Jump back to the right window
- Keep notes close to the work they belong to
- Restore busy workspaces faster
- Dim everything else when you need focus
- Capture long pages and keep the result easy to use

## Privacy

Waindow is built for Mac-side work. Window content stays on your Mac. The app contacts the licensing service for trial, checkout, license activation, refresh, and recovery.
